Privacy, in plain English

Kenoki runs on your Mac. Projects are saved locally in your Documents/Kenoki folder. We do not sell or share your data. By default, Kenoki sends no telemetry. Optional diagnostics (if enabled later) will always be opt-in.

You sign into providers (e.g., ChatGPT or Claude) inside the app. Credentials are stored by macOS Keychain. Kenoki cannot see your passwords.

If you uninstall Kenoki, delete the app and optionally remove Documents/Kenoki to delete your projects.
